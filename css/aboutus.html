Institution Overview

GZNIT, established as a tribute to G Akash, is a prestigious institution founded by Narender and Zaheer. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values essential for personal and professional growth. The institution fosters an environment that encourages academic excellence and innovation.

Chairman: Narender

Director: Zaheer

Principal: Subharaju Gokkaya

Since its inception, GZNIT has become a symbol of dedication and commitment, upholding the vision and values of its founders to make a lasting impact in education and technology.
